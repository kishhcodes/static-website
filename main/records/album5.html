Lust for Life

Lust for Life mixes numerous music styles. It was described by Stereogum as "Lana Del Rey's version of an A-list pop album, with a big budget and big-name contributors." Its sound was also described as "new-age folk" by Billboard and "trap pop" by Vulture.
